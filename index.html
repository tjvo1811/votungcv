Research

2025 — 2026 · Rice University

Qubit routing for quantum circuit optimization

Designed an integer-programming heuristic that maps logical qubits to hardware-constrained physical qubits while minimizing inserted SWAP gates. Benchmarked routing methods against SABRE and TKET on gate count and circuit depth.

Fall 2025 — present · University of St. Thomas

Competitive zero forcing

Studying a two-player extension of the classical zero-forcing color-change rule. The project brings together graph theory, vertex connectivity, and computational modeling with Dr. Mary Flagg.

View conference poster →

Summer 2026 · University of Puerto Rico, Ponce

Fubini rankings, parking functions, and Cayley permutations

Proved enumerative and structural results, characterized intersections among combinatorial families, and co-authored an article draft supported by Python enumeration.

2024 — 2025 · Lone Star College Honors College

Recruitment tactics and desertion during the Vietnam War

Examined how U.S. lottery-draft and Viet Cong propaganda recruitment tactics shaped soldier desertion rates, using primary-source posters and draft records.
