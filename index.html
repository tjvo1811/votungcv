Research

Here are some things I have researched.

  • Qubit routing for quantum circuit optimization

    • Rice University · 2025–26 Designed an integer-programming heuristic that maps logical qubits to hardware-constrained physical qubits while minimizing inserted SWAP gates.
    • Benchmarked routing methods against SABRE and TKET on gate count and circuit depth.

  • Competitive zero forcing

    • University of St. Thomas · Fall 2025–present Studying a two-player extension of the classical zero-forcing color-change rule with Dr. Mary Flagg.
    • Brings together graph theory, vertex connectivity, and computational modeling.
    • View conference poster →

  • Fubini rankings, parking functions, and Cayley permutations

    • University of Puerto Rico, Ponce · Summer 2026 Proved enumerative and structural results and characterized intersections among combinatorial families.
    • Co-authored an article draft supported by Python enumeration.

  • Recruitment tactics and desertion during the Vietnam War

    • Lone Star College Honors College · 2024–25 Examined how U.S. lottery-draft and Viet Cong propaganda recruitment tactics shaped soldier desertion rates.
    • Used primary-source posters and draft records.
    • Read paper → · View conference poster →

  • Air pollution and life expectancy

    • Lone Star College Honors College · 2024 Compared the United States and Chad using a Most Similar System Design case-study framework.
    • Identified socioeconomic status, infant mortality, healthcare access, and reforestation investment as important confounding factors.
    • Read paper →

  • Intercultural communication and leadership

    • Lone Star College Honors College · 2024 Authored an autoethnography of competing at National Model UN–New York.
    • Used triangulation and content analysis to examine public speaking and leadership in large delegate settings.
    • Read paper →
